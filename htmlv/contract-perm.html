ABOUT OUR CONTRACT & PERM SOLUTIONS

Clients all over the world know us as the search specialists they can rely on for their permanent and contract recruitment needs – even in the most challenging locations.

Clients all over the world know us as the search specialists they can rely on for their permanent and contract recruitment needs – even in the most challenging locations.

We understand that recruiters are trying to sell to you every day. We won’t do that. We see our clients as partners, not pound, euro or dollar signs. Our ethos is all about building long-term relationships, creating a community of clients and candidates, and becoming friends.

Whatever the size of your project or your company, you can turn to us. We value our shared relationship and will do everything we can to help you. Companies from tiny start-ups to world-leading giants trust us to fill the hardest-to-fill roles.

We have the experience and the connections it takes to find the best person for every job – even if you need them in 24 hours. And if you need a whole project team, we can assemble the top talent for you at short notice. That’s because our candidates trust us too.

We offer permanent, short-term, and short-term to permanent recruitment solutions. Regardless of which you choose, your satisfaction is our main concern, and we’re committed to fulfilling your needs.

Short-term solutions Short-term to Permanent Solutions Permanent Solutions

Short-term solutions, also known as interim or temporary contracts, are useful to cover busy periods, core projects, annual leave, maternity leave or long-term sickness.

We’ll handle all the admin and salary payments for the contractor, freeing up your time and energy. All you’ll need to do is pay a simple invoice at regular intervals. We’ll also stay in touch with both you and the contractor to make sure that you’re both happy.
