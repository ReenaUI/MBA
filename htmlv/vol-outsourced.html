What is a Statement of Work (SOW)?

A statement of work (SOW) is a document that describes a project’s requirements. It defines the deliverables, the scope of the work, the location, timelines, and payment terms and conditions. A good SOW is vital to avoid costly misunderstandings, disputes, and overspending.

Statement of work management means ensuring the services agreed in the SOW are delivered on time and on budget. This includes taking responsibility for risk management, special requirements, negotiating with suppliers, and creating cost savings and efficiencies.

SOWs are usually used with a:

Request for Proposal (RFP)

A document setting out the client’s requirements so the service provider can make an informed bid for the project.

Master Services Agreement (MSA)

A document covering terms and conditions for the entire working relationship, not just the project.

Performance-based

This clearly explains the project purpose and deliverables, and the resources that will be provided, but doesn’t specify how the work should be carried out. This type is the favourite because it’s flexible and shares risk evenly.

Project, advisory and analysis

We offer much more than recruitment services. Working with your in-house team, we’ll help you perfect your talent engagement strategy and empower your people to respond more effectively to whatever challenges and opportunities the future brings.

What are talent advisory services?

Talent advisory services (TAS) look beyond current vacancies to focus on your future needs. A good talent advisory partner will provide tailor-made services to fit those needs, whether you want to upskill your people, roll out a new recruitment technology, audit your workforce, or attract more and better talent.

What are the benefits of a talent advisory service?

Our services can help you transform both your permanent and interim/temporary recruitment, and support all your people in doing their most effective work. We can provide one-off specialist projects or combine several services into a bespoke package for you.

Areas we cover include workforce planning, employer branding, training and technology. Our services will help you counter challenges from competitors and maintain your edge. We use data-driven analysis of labour markets, skill sets, and economic factors to help you attract and retain the top talent even for the most niche and hard-to-fill roles. You’ll be able to make job offers with confidence, while saving time and money.

Talent recruitment and retention are the most critical factors in your success. If you’re ready to take yours to the next level, reach out today to benefit from our expertise.
